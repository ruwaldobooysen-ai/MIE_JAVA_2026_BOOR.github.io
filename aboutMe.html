About Me

Hello! My name is Ruwaldo Booysen, and I am an aspiring web developer with a strong passion for technology and digital design. I enjoy creating websites that are not only visually appealing but also structured, responsive, and easy to use.

I started learning web development out of curiosity, wanting to understand how websites are built and how they function behind the scenes. What began as simple experimentation quickly developed into a serious interest. Since then, I have been building my skills in HTML and CSS, focusing on clean code, modern layouts, and user-friendly design principles.

I am motivated by growth and self-improvement. Every project I work on is an opportunity to learn something new and refine my abilities. I enjoy problem-solving, improving performance, and continuously challenging myself to become better than I was yesterday.

My long-term goal is to build a successful career in software development, contribute to impactful projects, and continue expanding my knowledge in both front-end and back-end technologies.




My Interests

Outside of coding, I have a strong passion for music and gaming. I love playing the guitar and teaching myself how to play my favorite songs. I also enjoy competitive gaming and streaming my gameplay for an audience, which helps me develop communication skills and confidence.
